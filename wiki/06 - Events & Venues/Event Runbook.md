# Event Runbook

This is a placeholder runbook for future events. Do not treat it as complete until tested.

## Pre-Event

- Define event goal.
- Confirm owner and decision authority.
- Confirm venue and written terms.
- Confirm date, time, capacity, and costs.
- Confirm DJ lineup and set times.
- Confirm equipment and sound.
- Confirm promotion plan.
- Confirm ticketing or RSVP flow.
- Confirm filming and consent approach.
- Confirm safety, transport, and emergency contact plan.

## Event Day

- Load-in.
- Soundcheck.
- Door or RSVP check.
- Content capture.
- Set transitions.
- Issue log.
- Closeout.

## Post-Event

- Financial recap.
- Attendance recap.
- Content recap.
- Audience feedback.
- Venue feedback.
- Team retrospective.
- Decision: repeat, adjust, or stop.
