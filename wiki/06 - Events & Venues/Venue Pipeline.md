## Pipeline Stages

- Idea.
- Researched.
- Contact identified.
- Outreach sent.
- Conversation active.
- Site visit / meeting.
- Proposal sent.
- Confirmed.
- Rejected.
- Parked.

## Evaluation Criteria

- Music fit.
- Audience fit.
- Capacity.
- Sound system.
- DJ booth/setup.
- Filming permission.
- Location and transport.
- Safety.
- Existing event calendar.
- Cost or revenue-share model.
- Management responsiveness.
