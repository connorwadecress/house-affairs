# Event Strategy

## Strategic Progression

1. Recorded sets with no public attendance.
2. Small invited audience recordings.
3. House Affairs nights at existing venues.
4. Recurring ticketed events.
5. Long-term dedicated House Affairs venue or space.

## Minimum Viable Event Hypothesis

A small, curated House Affairs night at a Pretoria venue can validate whether the content audience converts into real attendance.

## Event Success Criteria

- Safe and well-run.
- Music direction is clear.
- Venue relationship improves.
- Audience feedback is captured.
- Costs and revenue are recorded.
- Content output strengthens the brand.

## Risks

- Venue mismatch.
- Poor turnout.
- Unclear revenue split.
- Music rights/licensing issues.
- Sound/noise restrictions.
- Safety and transport.
- Inadequate filming consent.

## Required Before Ticketed Events

- Written decision from the team.
- Venue agreement or written confirmation.
- Budget and break-even model.
- Compliance check.
- Promotion plan.
- Owner for event operations.
- Post-event reporting template.
