# Partnership Outreach

## Purpose

Create a repeatable way to approach venues, brands, creators, photographers, videographers, and community partners.

## Outreach Principles

- Be specific about what House Affairs is asking for.
- Do not overstate audience size.
- Lead with music fit, content quality, and community direction.
- Keep terms written.
- Record every meaningful conversation in [[00 - Meta/Correspondence Log|Correspondence Log]].

## Initial Venue Ask Hypotheses

- Permission to film a session at the venue during a quiet time.
- A curated House Affairs DJ night.
- A collaborative event with shared promotion.
- A trial night before recurring commitment.

## Required Evidence Before Outreach

- Official social links.
- Example session links.
- Short brand description.
- Confirmed public contact route or direct contact person.
- Proposed value to venue.
- Clear ask.
- Venue-specific unknowns to verify.
- Compliance questions relevant to the ask.

## Minimum Outreach Packet

- One-page House Affairs venue brief.
- Links to current House Affairs sessions.
- Specific ask with preferred date/window, setup needs, and filming needs.
- Evidence-backed reason the venue fits.
- Questions on sound, DJ setup, filming, rights/licensing responsibilities, safety, promotion, ticketing if relevant, and commercial terms.
- Internal note separating confirmed facts, assumptions, open questions, and founder preference.
