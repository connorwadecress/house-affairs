### Verbatim

> "House Affairs is a collective of like-minded DJs who like house music and want to bring house music to the public and to start off with the area and streets of Pretoria."

> "House Affairs is a play on South African Home Affairs, and the catchphrase here is 'House Affairs, get your affairs in order.'"

> "The whole idea is that we want to be able to create (this is the dream, this is the vision, long-term vision): we want to create an area, have a venue specifically for House Affairs DJs for people to come and appreciate house music and electronic music together in a place in Pretoria."

> "Pretoria has lots of party places, but all of which are the same, same music. It's very white girl music, it's very pop music, whereas we want to have house music like they have in Cape Town or like they have in Sanson, and we believe it's possible, but we understand it's going to be a long journey."

> "To start off with, House Affairs Collective has been releasing weekly videos and hour-long sets of DJs playing and mixing music together on the YouTube channel, and they have an Instagram account as well, House Affairs. This is also a registered business. We also own the domain. house-affairs.org"

> "The general roadmap is we're going to be creating weekly streams where different DJs, who are right now friends of ours, do DJ sets in locations. We want to explore locations like rooftops, sunsets, copies, hills, and beautiful areas, and record and make streams."

> "There's a lot of different YouTubers who do similar stuff like this. I really like Flavour Trip. Another one is Chris Luno, who does very similar."

> "The idea is that once we do enough of these videos, we create a following, and we can maybe start posting that we're going to be doing an event so people can come and be part of the videos in the sets."

> "We want to work with local Pretoria areas like Billy Boys or Mocha Club in Water Clue, where they have DJing, maybe even Lucky Rodrigo's, where we can have a night of DJing."

> "House Affairs Collective is the DJs, and we organise it. Ultimately, long-term, we actually have our own events where we can host our own events once every month or whatnot and get some money by selling tickets."

> "That's the idea, but we ultimately want to bring house music, the dream of house music, to Pretoria."

> "Main Founder and DJ: Gerhard Johan Kloppers (Kloppie for Short)
Co DJ: Cameron van Rooyen
Co DJ: Muller Dunnhauser
Digital Operations Lead and Amateur DJ: Connor Cress (ME)"

> "It's worth noting that every co-founder here is very, very technical and tech-driven and AI and agentech-driven. We don't need to simplify anything. We can actually go full agentech routing here: have agentech operations, agentech marketing, agentech management."
