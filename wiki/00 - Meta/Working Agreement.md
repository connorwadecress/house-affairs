## Operating Rules

1. Raw team voice is captured before it is polished.
2. Founder-specific opinions are not rewritten as collective consensus.
3. Assumptions are labeled.
4. Money, ownership, legal, customer, platform, and venue commitments require a written decision.
5. Venue facts, regulatory claims, and music rights claims require evidence before action.
6. Agents may propose aggressive automation, but must keep safety, access control, and auditability explicit.

## Review Rhythm

- **Weekly:** content pipeline, social performance, venue research, blockers, next sessions.
- **Monthly:** roadmap, audience growth, money, roles, outreach, event readiness.
- **Quarterly:** long-term strategy, business model, ownership, major investments, venue strategy.
