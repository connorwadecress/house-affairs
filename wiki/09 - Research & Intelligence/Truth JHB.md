# Truth JHB

## Question

What can House Affairs learn from Truth JHB as a mature Gauteng electronic-music venue model?

## Short Answer

Truth JHB is not a Pretoria target, but it is a strong reference for mature venue operations: recurring event series, multi-room programming, clear ticketing, location pages, terms, door policy, safety language, and production investment.

## Confirmed Facts

- Truth's official events page lists several 2026 events and recurring series, including Club Night, Stay True Sounds, Fresh House Flava, and 30 Years of Jon S.
- Truth's official location popup confirms Old Pretoria Rd, Midrand, Gauteng, South Africa, 1685.
- Truth's official terms page documents ticket/refund rules, age restriction, prohibited items, security checks, right of admission, VIP table policy, dress code, personal belongings, and safety policy.
- A Live Design Online article reports that Truth recently reopened with a state-of-the-art lighting setup and references five dance floors. This is a trade-media source, not a direct Truth source.

## Mechanics To Learn

- A serious venue makes rules visible before people arrive.
- Ticketing and refund policy are part of the product.
- Multiple rooms allow multiple music lanes under one venue umbrella.
- Recurring series can sit beside one-off guest/headliner events.
- Production quality becomes a differentiator when the venue is a club, not just a bar.

## What House Affairs Should Not Copy Blindly

- House Affairs is not yet a multi-room venue. Do not imitate large-club complexity for a first event.
- Do not assume Truth's safety, security, lighting, or ticketing infrastructure exists at smaller Pretoria venues.

Use Truth as the compliance and operations benchmark: House Affairs' first event plan should have a smaller version of the same clarity around age rules, ticket/refund terms, safety, prohibited items, and door responsibility.
