# WHYLY

## Question

What can House Affairs learn from WHYLY as a Pretoria dance-music reference?

## Short Answer

WHYLY is the strongest Pretoria-specific reference found in this sprint. Public ticketing and social evidence position it as a long-running Pretoria/Gauteng dance-music club night with residents, ticketing, guest artists, venue movement, and a clear "012 underground" identity.

## Confirmed Facts

- A 2023 Howler event page says WHYLY serves Pretoria and Gauteng dance-music culture, was founded in 2017, and operates as a club night.
- The same Howler page places a WHYLY event at 103 Club Avenue, Waterkloof, Pretoria with an 18+ age requirement.
- A 2026 Howler page lists WHYLY as the organiser for 909 Rave ft. YOZE at Truth Night Club in Midrand.
- Search/social snippets found during this sprint indicate WHYLY has celebrated 8 years and references Pretoria venues including Club Avenue and Heartfelt Arena, but those details should be treated as social discovery until verified with durable pages.

## Mechanics To Learn

- Build a recurring identity that can move between venues.
- Use residents as continuity anchors.
- Bring in regional/international guests to signal credibility.
- Use ticketing pages as public proof of programming, age rules, lineups, and venue choices.
- Preserve local language and scene specificity without becoming a generic "party brand".

## What House Affairs Should Not Copy Blindly

- Do not copy WHYLY's underground/techno positioning unless House Affairs intentionally chooses that lane.
- Do not assume WHYLY's audience, venue terms, or compliance setup will transfer to House Affairs.
- Do not use WHYLY claims in public copy unless sourced and relevant.
