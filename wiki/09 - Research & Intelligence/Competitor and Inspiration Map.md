## Rule

Inspiration is not strategy. Extract mechanics, then adapt them to Pretoria and House Affairs.

## First Sprint Lessons

- **Recurring formats matter:** Mature references are built around repeatable nights or series.
- **Residents create trust:** WHYLY, TOYTOY, and house series examples use known residents/hosts as continuity.
- **Rules are part of the brand:** Age, door, parking, refund, safety, and filming rules are visible in mature event ecosystems.
- **House Affairs must stay content-aware:** Some underground references restrict phones/photos, which conflicts with House Affairs' current media engine unless event formats are separated.
