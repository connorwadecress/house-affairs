# Strategic Objectives

## Objective 1 - Build a Repeatable Content Engine

- **Why now:** Content is the current proven activity and the lowest-friction way to build audience.
- **Success condition:** Weekly sessions can be planned, shot, edited, published, and promoted without reinventing the workflow.
- **Evidence needed:** Publishing cadence, production checklist, platform analytics, team ownership.

## Objective 2 - Prove Audience Pull

- **Why now:** Events and venue partnerships need evidence of demand.
- **Success condition:** Measurable engagement, returning viewers, followers, comments, DMs, signups, or attendance intent.
- **Evidence needed:** YouTube analytics, Instagram insights, mailing list/signups, qualitative feedback.

## Objective 3 - Build a Pretoria Venue Pipeline

- **Why now:** The roadmap depends on local venues that can host house-focused nights or filmed sessions.
- **Success condition:** Verified list of venues with contacts, fit notes, constraints, and outreach status.
- **Evidence needed:** Official venue details, contact records, correspondence, meeting notes.

## Objective 4 - Define the Event Path

- **Why now:** The jump from content to offline events introduces money, legal, brand, and operations risk.
- **Success condition:** Clear minimum viable event model, roles, budget, compliance checklist, and risk controls.
- **Evidence needed:** Event runbook, budget model, licensing research, venue agreement template, decision log.

## Objective 5 - Operate Agentically Without Losing Control

- **Why now:** The team is technical and wants agentic operations from the start.
- **Success condition:** Agents can research, draft, plan, summarize, and update notes while humans retain authority over commitments.
- **Evidence needed:** Working Agreement, task workflow, source logs, validation requirements.
