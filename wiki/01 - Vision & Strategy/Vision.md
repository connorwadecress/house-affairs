# Vision

## Long-Term Vision

House Affairs becomes Pretoria's trusted house and electronic music collective: a recognizable content brand, a reliable event organiser, a home for local DJs, and eventually a dedicated venue or space where people gather for house music culture.

## Strategic Story

Pretoria has nightlife, but House Affairs believes there is room for a more intentional house music movement. The first proof point is not a venue. It is a repeatable media engine: DJs, locations, recorded sets, and community touchpoints. Once attention and trust exist, the collective can invite people into recordings, partner with venues, run House Affairs nights, and later host recurring ticketed events.

## Confirmed From Source

- Pretoria is the starting geography.
- The music focus is house and electronic music.
- The early vehicle is weekly recorded DJ sessions.
- The venue dream is long-term and acknowledged as a long journey.
- The team is comfortable with technical, AI, and agentic operations.

## Vision Constraints

- Do not present the dedicated venue as current reality.
- Do not present venue partnerships as agreed before evidence exists.
- Do not claim market demand before research or audience data exists.
- Do not claim legal, licensing, or event compliance readiness before verification.
