# Mission

## Draft Mission

House Affairs exists to bring credible, community-driven house music culture to Pretoria through DJ sessions, recorded sets, events, and eventually a dedicated space for house and electronic music.

## Confirmed Context

- **Confirmed:** House Affairs is intended as a collective of like-minded DJs who like house music and want to bring house music to Pretoria. Source: [[00 - Meta/Founding Story - Verbatim|Founding Story - Verbatim]].
- **Confirmed:** The long-term dream is a venue or area for House Affairs DJs and fans to appreciate house and electronic music together in Pretoria. Source: [[00 - Meta/Founding Story - Verbatim|Founding Story - Verbatim]].
- **Confirmed:** Current activity includes weekly videos/hour-long DJ sets on YouTube and Instagram presence. Source: founder statement and screenshot evidence in [[00 - Meta/Source Materials|Source Materials]].

## Assumptions

- Pretoria has unmet demand for more house-focused nightlife experiences.
- Content can create enough audience momentum to support offline events.
- House Affairs should present itself as inclusive, credible, music-first, and locally grounded.

## Open Questions

- What exact community does House Affairs want to serve first: DJs, house listeners, students, young professionals, venue regulars, or a broader Pretoria nightlife audience?
- What does "house music" include and exclude for House Affairs programming?
- What should success look like in the first 90 days?
