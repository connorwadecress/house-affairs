# Roadmap

## Phase 0 - Project Brain and Source Capture

- **Status:** Active.
- **Why now:** Future work needs a reliable operating memory.
- **Owner:** Connor.
- **Success condition:** A new agent or team member can read the wiki and understand the vision, evidence, open questions, and next actions.
- **Validation:** Home, source materials, open questions, decisions, and department notes exist and cross-link.

## Phase 1 - Weekly Session Engine

- **Status:** Started, based on founder statement.
- **Why now:** Weekly recorded DJ sets are the current operating wedge.
- **Owner:** TBD.
- **Success condition:** Sessions are produced and published on a predictable cadence.
- **Dependencies:** [[04 - Content & Media/Weekly DJ Sets Workflow|Weekly DJ Sets Workflow]], platform access, location plan, roster availability.
- **Validation:** Published links, production checklist, analytics snapshot.

## Phase 2 - Audience and Community Signal

- **Status:** Not started as a measured workstream.
- **Why now:** Venue and event expansion should be backed by audience evidence.
- **Owner:** TBD.
- **Success condition:** House Affairs tracks followers, views, watch time, comments, DMs, email/signups, and attendance intent.
- **Dependencies:** [[03 - Audience & Community/Community Strategy|Community Strategy]], [[10 - Product & Engineering/Data Model|Data Model]]
- **Validation:** Monthly audience report.

## Phase 3 - Audience-Attended Recordings

- **Status:** Future.
- **Why now:** This bridges pure content and full events.
- **Owner:** TBD.
- **Success condition:** Small invited audience attends a recording safely and the output strengthens the brand.
- **Dependencies:** location permissions, guest management, safety, release consent, audio/video workflow.
- **Validation:** Post-event review, content output, audience feedback.

## Phase 4 - Venue Nights and Partnerships

- **Status:** Future.
- **Why now:** Venue nights test demand without owning a venue.
- **Owner:** TBD.
- **Success condition:** One or more Pretoria venues host House Affairs DJ nights.
- **Dependencies:** [[06 - Events & Venues/Venue Pipeline|Venue Pipeline]], [[06 - Events & Venues/Partnership Outreach|Partnership Outreach]], compliance review.
- **Validation:** Signed agreement or written venue confirmation, attendance, financial recap.

## Phase 5 - Recurring Ticketed Events

- **Status:** Future.
- **Why now:** Ticketed events create revenue and prove operating capability.
- **Owner:** TBD.
- **Success condition:** Monthly or periodic House Affairs events run with clear budgets, promotion, operations, and post-event reporting.
- **Dependencies:** audience signal, venue partner, legal/compliance, ticketing, finance controls.
- **Validation:** Event P&L, attendance, retention, audience feedback.

## Phase 6 - Dedicated House Affairs Venue or Space

- **Status:** Long-term dream.
- **Why now:** Not now; it is the strategic north star.
- **Owner:** Founding team.
- **Success condition:** A viable business case, funding model, location, operations model, legal setup, and audience base exist.
- **Dependencies:** sustained demand, finances, operations, licensing, lease/property strategy.
- **Validation:** Formal business plan and team decision.
