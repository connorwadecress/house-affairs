# North Star Narrative

House Affairs starts with a simple belief: Pretoria can have a stronger house music culture than the current nightlife default.

The first expression is recorded DJ sessions: friends, DJs, locations, and hour-long sets that show the sound and the feeling. The second expression is community: people begin to follow, comment, share, attend, and identify with the movement. The third expression is events: venue nights, invited recordings, and ticketed gatherings. The long-term expression is a dedicated place for House Affairs and Pretoria's house music community.

This narrative is internal strategy, not final public copy.

## Guardrails

- Keep the sound and community at the center.
- Build credibility through consistency.
- Do not overclaim scale before audience data exists.
- Do not treat the venue dream as a short-term promise.
- Translate raw internal frustration with mainstream nightlife into constructive public positioning.
