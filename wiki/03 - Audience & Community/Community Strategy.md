# Community Strategy

## Baseline Community Path

1. Publish consistent DJ sessions.
2. Make every session easy to share.
3. Invite comments, track feedback, and respond.
4. Build a lightweight owned audience channel, such as email or WhatsApp, after platform access is confirmed.
5. Invite a small trusted group to recordings.
6. Use audience signal to support venue nights and ticketed events.

## Community Principles

- Music-first.
- Pretoria-rooted.
- Inclusive without becoming generic.
- Consistent enough to build trust.
- Small and credible before large and diluted.

## Open Questions

- Should the first owned community channel be email, WhatsApp, Discord, Instagram broadcast channel, or another channel?
- Who moderates community channels?
- What is the minimum privacy and consent standard for filming attendees?
