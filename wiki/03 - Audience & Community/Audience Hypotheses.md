# Audience Hypotheses

These are starting hypotheses. They need research and platform data.

## Potential Early Audiences

- Pretoria house music listeners who feel underserved by mainstream nightlife.
- Friends and extended networks of the founding DJs.
- Local DJs and producers looking for a credible community platform.
- Young professionals and students who want electronic music events with better music direction.
- People who follow similar creator formats, such as destination DJ sets and scenic sessions.

## Needs To Validate

- Which audience actually watches the videos.
- Which audience would attend recordings.
- Which audience would pay for events.
- Which locations and time slots feel safest and most attractive.
- Which subgenres of house resonate with the founding audience.

## Data To Capture

- YouTube subscribers, views, watch time, retention, comments, traffic sources.
- Instagram followers, reach, profile visits, saves, shares, DMs.
- Email or WhatsApp signup intent.
- Event attendance intent.
- Qualitative feedback after each session.
