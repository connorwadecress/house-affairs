# Brand Foundations

## Name

House Affairs.

## Name Meaning

House Affairs plays off South African Home Affairs while centering house music.

## Catchphrase

"House Affairs, get your affairs in order."

## Draft Positioning

House Affairs is a Pretoria house music collective creating recorded sessions, community moments, and future events for people who want a more intentional house and electronic music experience.

## Screenshot-Derived Public Bio

"Modern house music. Real connections. Sessions - Events - Community"

## YouTube About-Derived Positioning

Source: [[04 - Content & Media/YouTube Snapshot - 2026-07-01]]

- Community-driven electronic music movement.
- Founded in Pretoria, South Africa.
- Focused on modern house music culture.
- Built around curated sounds, DJ sets, live recordings, artist showcases, event content, and the House Affairs movement.
- Uses "Get Your Affairs In Order" as public call-to-action language.

## Brand Signals

- Pretoria-rooted.
- Modern house music.
- Real connections.
- Sessions, events, and community.
- Black-and-white HA visual identity visible in screenshot.

## Public Language Guardrails

- Use constructive language about the gap in Pretoria nightlife.
- Avoid insulting audience groups or reducing the market to stereotypes.
- Do not claim to be the only house music movement in Pretoria unless research proves it.
- Do not claim partnerships, venue ownership, or event scale before evidence exists.
