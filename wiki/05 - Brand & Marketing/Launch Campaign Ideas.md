# Launch Campaign Ideas

These are ideas, not committed campaigns.

## Campaign Themes

- Get Your Affairs In Order.
- Pretoria After Hours.
- Sessions From the Capital.
- House Has an Address.
- The House Affairs Department.

## Content Ideas

- Session teasers before each release.
- DJ profile posts.
- Location reveal clips.
- Short mix moments.
- "Affairs in order" countdowns for events.
- Audience invite posts once attended recordings are ready.

## Validation

Test campaign language through:

- Saves and shares.
- Comments and DMs.
- Click-through to YouTube.
- Signups or attendance interest.
- Founder approval.
