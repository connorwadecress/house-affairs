# Operating Cadence

## Weekly Rhythm

- Review last session and analytics.
- Confirm next session DJ, location, and production plan.
- Update social/content calendar.
- Review venue/research tasks.
- Capture decisions and open questions.
- Update [[Task Board]].

## Monthly Rhythm

- Review audience growth and content performance.
- Review venue pipeline.
- Review business/legal/finance open questions.
- Decide next month priorities.
- Review team load and role clarity.

## Quarterly Rhythm

- Revisit roadmap.
- Revisit business model hypotheses.
- Revisit event readiness.
- Revisit ownership, compensation, and decision authority if relevant.
