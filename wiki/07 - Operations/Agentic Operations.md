# Agentic Operations

## Intent

House Affairs can use AI and agents deeply because the founding team is technical and comfortable with agentic workflows.

## Agent Workstreams

- Research Pretoria venues and nightlife references.
- Summarize meetings and correspondence.
- Draft outreach messages.
- Maintain wiki notes.
- Build campaign calendars.
- Analyze platform metrics when exports are provided.
- Prepare event runbooks and checklists.
- Support website, automation, and internal tools.

## Human Approval Required

Agents must not independently:

- Send external messages.
- Commit money.
- Sign or accept agreements.
- Publish public posts.
- Change passwords or platform access.
- Make legal or compliance conclusions.
- Claim verified facts without source evidence.

## Agent Safety Rules

- Every output should cite the source note or evidence used.
- Unverified claims stay labeled as assumptions.
- External research must include source URLs and retrieval dates.
- Secrets stay out of the wiki.
- Access changes require human approval and an audit trail.
