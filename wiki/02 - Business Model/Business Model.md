# Business Model

## Current Business Stage

House Affairs is in foundation and audience-building mode.

The current business model is not yet proven. The current activity is content production and brand/community formation.

## Baseline Model Hypothesis

1. Produce consistent house music sessions.
2. Build audience and brand trust.
3. Convert audience signal into venue partnerships and attended recordings.
4. Run House Affairs nights at partner venues.
5. Develop recurring ticketed events.
6. Evaluate dedicated venue or space once demand, operations, and finances are proven.

## Potential Value Propositions

- For listeners: a credible Pretoria house music experience and community.
- For DJs: a platform to perform, be recorded, and reach local audiences.
- For venues: curated DJ nights and promotion to an engaged audience.
- For sponsors/partners: access to a specific music and nightlife community.

## What Is Not Yet Proven

- Willingness to pay for tickets.
- Venue partner economics.
- Audience size and audience profile.
- Content monetization potential.
- Sponsorship fit.
- Event margin.
- Legal and licensing requirements.
