# Revenue Hypotheses

These are hypotheses, not current revenue facts.

## Possible Revenue Streams

| Stream | Description | Evidence status | Validation path |
|---|---|---|---|
| Ticketed events | Charge entry for House Affairs nights or monthly events. | Founder stated long-term idea. | Pilot event P&L and attendance. |
| Venue partnership fees | Venue pays or shares revenue for curated DJ night. | Assumption. | Venue conversations and written offers. |
| Bar revenue share | Revenue share with venue for event-driven bar sales. | Assumption. | Venue agreement and event recap. |
| Sponsorship | Local brands support sessions/events. | Assumption. | Sponsor outreach and rate-card testing. |
| Content monetization | YouTube or platform revenue. | Assumption. | Platform eligibility and analytics. |
| Merchandise | Branded items tied to community identity. | Assumption. | Small preorder test. |
| Private bookings | DJs booked under House Affairs brand. | Assumption. | Inquiry tracking and booking terms. |

## Guardrails

- Do not forecast revenue without a source.
- Do not publish ticket prices before costs and audience willingness are tested.
- Do not commit to revenue share or sponsor deliverables without written approval.
