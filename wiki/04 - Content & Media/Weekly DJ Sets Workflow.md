# Weekly DJ Sets Workflow

## Purpose

Create a repeatable process for planning, recording, publishing, and learning from weekly House Affairs sessions.

## Draft Workflow

1. Choose DJ and set direction.
2. Choose location and confirm permission/safety.
3. Confirm equipment, power, audio, cameras, storage, and backup.
4. Record the set.
5. Edit audio/video and create artwork.
6. Publish to YouTube.
7. Promote on Instagram.
8. Capture links, metrics, public comments, requests, and lessons in the wiki.

## Evidence To Capture Per Session

- Date and location.
- DJ(s).
- Set length.
- Published links.
- Tracklist if available and legally appropriate.
- Production notes.
- Permission/consent status.
- Analytics snapshot.
- Public comment/request snapshot.
- Lessons for next session.

## Risks To Resolve

- Music rights and platform takedowns.
- Filming permissions.
- Attendee consent once people are visible.
- Weather, power, sound, and safety.
- Equipment failure.

## Audio QA Step

Added after public feedback on Session 002:

- Check stereo output before export.
- Check loudness against a reference session.
- Listen on headphones and speakers before publishing.
- Confirm the uploaded YouTube version does not sound muffled.
- Record any known audio compromise in the session note.
