# System Vision

## Long-Term System

House Affairs can become a highly agentic operating system for music, content, venues, and community.

## Potential Components

- Public website on `house-affairs.org`.
- Content archive for sessions and DJs.
- Venue pipeline CRM.
- Event planning workspace.
- Analytics dashboard.
- Social content calendar.
- Audience/community database.
- Agentic research and correspondence workflow.
- Internal wiki as source of truth.

## Current Reality

- No application code exists yet.
- The repository currently prioritizes the wiki and operating memory.
- Domain ownership is claimed but not verified in the repo.

## Design Principle

Build operational clarity before building software. The first product is the process.
