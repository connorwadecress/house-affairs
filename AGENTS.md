# Agent Instructions

This repository uses `wiki/` as the House Affairs project brain.

Before doing substantive work:

1. Read `wiki/Home.md`.
2. Read the topic-relevant notes linked from Home.
3. Check `wiki/00 - Meta/Open Questions.md` for unresolved blockers.
4. Check `wiki/00 - Meta/Joint Decisions Log.md` for prior decisions.
5. Check `wiki/00 - Meta/Source Materials.md` for the evidence trail.
6. Separate confirmed facts, assumptions, open questions, and founder-specific opinions.

While working:

- Do not invent production details, credentials, endpoints, schemas, versions, financials, venue agreements, follower counts, registration details, or test results.
- Prefer primary sources: founder statements, source notes, contracts, official docs, screenshots, logs, code, reproducible tests, and verified platform records.
- Treat chat context as source material only after it is captured in the wiki.
- Do not rewrite one founder or team member's view as joint consensus.
- Record meaningful decisions in `wiki/00 - Meta/Joint Decisions Log.md`.
- Record unresolved decision-relevant unknowns in `wiki/00 - Meta/Open Questions.md`.
- Record important messages, calls, meetings, and direction changes in `wiki/00 - Meta/Correspondence Log.md`.
- Record external research sources in `wiki/00 - Meta/Source Materials.md` or the relevant research note.
- Keep public-facing language separate from raw founding language when the raw wording is not suitable for brand use.

Before ending:

- Update the relevant wiki notes with what changed.
- Update `wiki/Home.md` if an important note, status, or next action changed.
- Record validation steps and results honestly.
- Leave the next person with a clear current status and next action.

Core rule:

> The wiki is the source of operational truth. If reality and the wiki disagree, fix the wiki or fix the work.
